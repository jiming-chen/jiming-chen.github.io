Lecture 3: Electric Potential Energy and Electric Fields

Electric Potential Energy If you have a charges $q_1$ and $q_2$ infinitely far away, and you want to bring them together so that they are $r_{12}$ apart, work has to be done. Work is $$\int \mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{s} = \int_{\infty}^{r_{12}} -\frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0} \frac{q_1q_2}{r_{12}^2}dr = \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0} \frac{q_1q_2}{r_{12}}.$$ Also, recall that work is path independent.
